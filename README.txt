OwnTracks
Collects and presents data sent by OwnTracks iOS and Androis apps.

From the OwnTracks web site (http://owntracks.org):

"Your location companion. OwnTracks allows you to keep track of your own
location. You can build your private location diary or share it with your
family and friends. OwnTracks is open-source and uses open protocols for
communication so you can be sure your data stays secure and private."
